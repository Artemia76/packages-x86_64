New kernels

A new kernel is finaly available, we move to the kernel 3.19.4, blocking problems on the first versions are gone.

A new long term support kernel from serie 3.18 is also available. It can be installed in parallel on the standard kernel. It's just two links to adjust in the boot folder. (initrd and kernel)

Next ISO

The openssh service will not be implemented in the iso anymore, this is an easy way to connect remotly if the password is too week. Even more when the router is not doing it's firewall job.

A new article explain how to produce it's own ISO to make a deploy very easy.

The installation script install-saravane available

install-saravane is working again. Be aware they are no kernel installed. It checks the minimum requirements: uptodate bash, gawk and wget

Stop prt-get / pkgutils support

I stop the support of cards and prt-get/pkgutils in parallel. The ports of crux are compatible with cards but not the other way around. Under NuTyX, we keep the doc, we keep man pages devel in separates sub packages. If we use the ports of NuTyX with pkgutils, we quickly end up with big packages (kernel for ex ) and then the DB of pkgutils get saturated which make pkgutils very slow.

The other problem is that the pre an post install are not names similar under cards and pkgutils, under pkgutils, they are named without the name of the package (or subpackage).
